Support Organizations for Refugees and Emergencies
Major International Organizations
UNHCR
The United Nations Refugee Agency provides protection and assistance to refugees worldwide.
ICRC
The International Committee of the Red Cross offers humanitarian help in conflict zones and emergencies.
UNICEF
The United Nations Children's Fund supports children and families in crisis situations.
MSF
Médecins Sans Frontières (Doctors Without Borders) delivers medical care in emergencies.
IRC
The International Rescue Committee helps people affected by humanitarian crises to survive and rebuild.
CARE International
CARE International fights global poverty and provides disaster relief.
Save the Children
Save the Children works to improve the lives of children in need around the world.
HIAS
HIAS supports refugees and asylum seekers with protection and advocacy.
Médecins du Monde
Doctors of the World provides medical care to vulnerable populations.
Additional Global Organizations
Refugees International
Refugees International advocates for lifesaving assistance and protection for displaced people.
IOM
The International Organization for Migration promotes humane and orderly migration for all.
Global Refuge (LIRS)
Global Refuge (formerly LIRS) supports refugees and migrants in rebuilding their lives.
UNRWA
The United Nations Relief and Works Agency provides support to Palestinian refugees.
Danish Refugee Council (DRC)
The Danish Refugee Council assists refugees and internally displaced persons worldwide.
IRAP
The International Refugee Assistance Project provides legal aid to refugees and displaced people.
ACTED
ACTED delivers humanitarian aid and supports sustainable development in crisis areas.
